Introduction to Pipetting Homepage

The most commonly used laboratory apparatus is the pipette. Medical laboratory professional are trained to demonstrate manual dexterity for experiments, whether it's to pipette 1 uL into a 96-well plate or to measure reagents to calibrator material. This class will teach (or retrain, you research folks) the theoretic background behind the pipette's mechanics and the proper handling of this apparatus.
